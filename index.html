King Sejong the Great

The Inventor of 한글

King Sejong the Great invented Hangul in 1443, creating one of the most scientific writing systems ever devised.

Hangul's design reflects the actual shape of the mouth and tongue when producing sounds, making it uniquely intuitive for learners. Each letter is a visual representation of how the sound is formed.
